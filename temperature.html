Comparison of temperature to latitude


For most people, it's common knowledge that the closer a location is to the equator, the warmer it is. It is important, however, that we definitively confirm these types of assumptions since they're the basis for so many other things.

This visualization presents a visual comparison of the maximum temperature of a location to its latitude. The maximum temperature is used to emphasize just how extreme the equatorial climate can be, and focuses on the northern hemisphere.

Analysis: There is a clear correlation between the maximum temperature and latitude.
